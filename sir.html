SIR Model: Simulating Disease Spread

This interactive model allows users to explore how the Basic Reproduction Number (R₀) influences the spread of infectious disease over time. You can observe trends in the Susceptible (S), Infected (I), and Recovered (R) populations across varying outbreak durations.

Simulation parameters are based on Bayesian estimates from Osthus et al. (2017) and foundational models by Cohen, Keeling, and Rothman (2008).
